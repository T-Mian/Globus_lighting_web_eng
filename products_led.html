Globus Lighting – LED luminaires and lighting systems

In Globus Lighting you will find Industrial LED luminaires, as well as for offices, architecture, streets, indoor facilities and sport. Globus Lighting LED luminaires are characterised by the highest quality, durability and energy-efficiency. Thanks to our LED lighting you will cut your costs up to 70% in comparison with traditional lighting Our outdoor and indoor lighting is inteligent lighting, which thanks to many kinds of sensors and control systems is problem-free and cost-effective. For example, thanks to motion sensorsthe light turns on when there are people in the room and thanks to dusk sensors, when it is not possible to work without additional light. All this is possible without switching on and manual control.

Available LED luminaires

Industrial luminaires

In our assortment you will find industrial lighting, which are particularly suitable for industrial halls and warehouses. Among the industrial luminaires we can mention linear industrial luminaires BSL (30-60-90),linear industrial luminaires BSL (120-150), workplace industrial luminaires 24 VDC/AVC, industrial luminaires High Bay BSL 020, industrial luminaires High Bay 030,industrial luminaires High Bay BSL, industrial luminaires Herbu and much more.

Outdoor luminaires and lighting systems

In Globus Lighting you can order also outdoor luminaires and lighting systems. Among our outdoor luminaires and lighting systems there are floodlights, park luminaires and street luminaires Globus Lighting floodlights are outdoor luminaires, but it is also possible to use them indoor, for example in sport facilities. This kind of lighting has a wide range of power from 50W to 100W, what makes them useful in many places. Other types of our outdoor lighting are park and street luminaires.

Downlight LED luminaires

Our downlight LED luminaires are for example DLA Downlight, Downlight and DGS Downlight. Downlight LED luminaires have a wide range of applications, for example as Office lighting, hotel lighting As well as lighting in Shopping centres. All Of our intelligent lightings systems are the best in places where automation is important.
