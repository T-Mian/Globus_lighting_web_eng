Lighting with 1-10V control

A lready during the design process, the client was offered to control LED luminaires so that the new production halls would not only be adequately lit, but also be economical in maintenance, and thanks to additional lighting sensors, they would automatically react to potential lighting shortages.
1-10V dimming gives us a lot, among other things, it helps us save energy by allowing us to adjust the brightness of the lighting, when we do not need full lighting, we can easily reduce the brightness of the lights to save energy. Additionally, 1-10V dimmer control provides a comprehensive lighting control solution, allowing you to adjust brightness of lights to suit specific needs. You can use a 1-10V dimmer to get the right lighting for your needs. Whether you need full light to get the job done, soft ambient light to relax, or something in between. It's also incredibly easy to use, with simple controls that let you easily adjust the brightness of the lights (a simple and intuitive way to control), and many many others.

At the end of the work

T he final stage of implementation was the appropriate setting of the control system - Globus Lighting specialists measured the lighting intensity. After analyzing the results, the luminous flux was reduced so as to maintain the desired lighting parameters while reducing the power of the luminaires. The modernized lighting met the customer's expectations, providing an average lighting intensity of 300 lx. Equally important was the positive opinion of the warehouse employees, who noted that their work comfort had significantly improved.
