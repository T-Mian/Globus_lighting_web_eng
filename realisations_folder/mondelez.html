Implementation for Mondelez - a food manufacturer

Lamps in HACCP conditions, with PZH certificate for the food industry.

A t Globus Lighting , we create modern LED lighting for industrial, office and street investments. This time we implemented LED lighting with control for the Mondelez warehouse in Skarbimierz, near Brzeg and Wrocław.



Implementation of LED lighting for Mondelez near Wrocław - lamps used


T o make the LED lighting used in Mondelez near Wrocław as effective, modern and energy-saving as possible, we used High Bay LED 140 W, BSL 40 W and BSL 60 W BELUS lamps.
All LED luminaires made for Mondelez near Wrocław were used under HACAP conditions, with the National Institute of Hygiene certificate, while maintaining stringent requirements resulting from restrictions for the food industry.





H igh Bay LED is an LED lamp that fits perfectly into industrial applications and replaces conventional light sources in rooms over 4 m high. High Bay LED lamps are highly efficient and long-lasting, which allows both saving electricity and reducing costs related to lighting maintenance and replacement. The LED fixtures use Philips Luxeon diodes, which increase the light efficiency of High Bay LED lamps.


B SL fixtures are tight beams of various lengths and power. These are industrial luminaires designed to illuminate production halls, warehouses and workplaces in rooms up to 8 meters high. The housing of the tight LED beam acts as a heat sink and ensures the optimal temperature for the operation of the LEDs, which increases their service life.



LED lighting and lamps at Mondelez near Wrocław – summary of the implementation


T he demand for electricity in the Mondelez warehouse located near Wrocław before the modernization of LED lighting was 28.87 kW. After modernizing the LED lamps, the demand dropped to 8.5 kW, which resulted in savings of 20.37 kW and a cost reduction of over 60%.
In addition to the new LED luminaires, motion sensors were also installed at Mondelez near Wrocław, which illuminate only at 30% power in the absence of an employee.
