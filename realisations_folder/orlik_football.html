LED lamps used to illuminate Orlik in Opole


W e used ULE and FL LED lamps to illuminate Orlik in Opole. This project was carried out for the needs of a sports facility. When designing sports facilities, the PN-EN 12193 standard should be followed, which specifies lighting requirements for indoor and outdoor sports events in Europe. It only takes into account artificial lighting. It contains guidelines for designing and controlling lighting installations for sports facilities, taking into account several factors: intensity, uniformity of lighting, glare limitations, and color properties of the light source.

The LED lamps we use in Opole have a control option that increases the light intensity when it gets dark.
