LED lamps used by Stradom from Częstochowa - types of luminaires

I n the implementation of LED lighting for the Stradom company from Częstochowa, tight LED beams of the Belus type were used. These are industrial luminaires intended primarily for production halls and warehouses. The casing of this lighting fixture is made of aluminum alloy and acts as a heat sink, which provides the LEDs with optimal temperature and contributes to their longer life. The lighting element is based on the use of a linear module with a length of 28 cm, thanks to which you can adjust the length of the fixture as well as the amount of light emitted to specific tasks.


LED lighting for Stradom in Częstochowa - description of the implementation

T he implementation of LED lighting at Stradom in Częstochowa involved the modernization of existing lighting and the expansion of the production hall. Belus BSL luminaires and Dali control were used.

Thanks to the modernization of LED lamps, the Stradom company from Częstochowa saved 70% of its current energy expenses. LED luminaires with Dali control are managed via a central control panel. The lighting dims and brightens itself depending on the intensity of natural light, which allows you to generate energy savings and extend the life of the fixtures.
