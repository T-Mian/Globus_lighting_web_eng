Explosion-proof LED lamps as lighting for hazardous areas


E xplosion-proof LED lamps are used in places with increased risk - in places where there is a risk of explosion. The use of explosion-proof LED lamps is a guarantee of durability and versatility. Our ATEX luminaires ensure work safety in a wide range of temperatures intended for zones 1/21 and 2/22. Explosion-proof luminaires are manufactured in accordance with ISO 9001 and the I i II classes. Their durability is up to 4 kV.
